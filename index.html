Marvera Group

Strategic Vision Behind Global Excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2000 in Switzerland, Marvera Group was born out of a vision to create a company that seamlessly bridges global markets with precision, integrity, and innovation.

Our name, a fusion of “Mar” (sea) and “Vera” (truth), reflects our commitment to honesty, transparency, and international reach — values that remain at the core of everything we do.

Our Mission

Marvera Group exists to enable excellence behind the scenes. We provide strategic oversight, operational infrastructure, and global governance that allow our partner companies to deliver world-class services without compromise.

Relationship with Hafen Consolidators

Hafen Consolidators is our operational front, specialising in freight forwarding, NVOCC services, and international trading. While Hafen manages the complex logistics, client relations, and commercial execution, Marvera Group operates strategically behind the scenes.

We ensure that the business is aligned with European-style precision, long-term planning, and disciplined operational standards. Think of it as Hafen being the engine, while Marvera is the navigation system: guiding, supporting, and safeguarding the company’s growth and reputation.

Global Presence & Operations

From our European roots in Switzerland, Marvera Group oversees an internationally distributed team. Despite geographical distance, all teams operate under a single disciplined ethos, valuing clarity, accountability, and professionalism.

  • Africa: Coordinating regional logistics.
  • Philippines: Specialists in commercial operations.
  • Eastern Europe: Supporting freight and trading operations.
  • LATAM: Regional growth support.

Our Philosophy

At Marvera Group, we believe in discipline over haste, clarity over chaos, and sustainable growth over quick wins. We are the quiet force behind visible success.

We handle the strategic, administrative, and infrastructural details so that our partners can focus on what they do best: connecting businesses worldwide and moving cargo efficiently.
